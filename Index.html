Policies & Warranty

Claytor Mobile Auto Repair is an independent mobile automotive repair service. All services are performed by an ASE-Certified technician using professional standards and industry-accepted repair practices.

All prices provided are estimates and may change due to vehicle condition, accessibility, rust, prior repairs, or additional issues discovered during service. Diagnostic fees are non-refundable but may be applied toward approved repairs.

Customer-supplied parts are installed at the customer’s request and are not covered under any labor or parts warranty. Claytor Mobile Auto Repair is not responsible for defects, failures, delays, or damages related to customer-supplied components.

While every effort is made to properly diagnose and repair vehicles, automotive systems are complex and additional issues may arise. Claytor Mobile Auto Repair is not responsible for pre-existing conditions, manufacturer defects, or unrelated failures occurring before or after service.

Payment is due upon completion of services unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

Limited Labor Warranty

Claytor Mobile Auto Repair provides a 30-day limited labor warranty from the date of service on workmanship only.

This warranty covers labor performed by Claytor Mobile Auto Repair if the original repair fails solely due to improper workmanship. Covered labor will be corrected at no additional charge, at the technician’s discretion.

This Labor Warranty Does NOT Cover:

  • Parts failure of any kind (new, used, aftermarket, OEM, or customer-supplied)
  • Customer-supplied parts or components
  • Normal wear-and-tear items
  • Pre-existing conditions or unrelated failures
  • Electrical, computer, or intermittent issues not present at the time of service
  • Rusted, seized, or previously damaged components
  • Vehicle modifications or poor maintenance history
  • Repairs altered or touched by another shop or individual after service
  • Diagnostic conclusions or scan results
  • Misuse, neglect, accidents, racing, towing overloads, or off-road use

Additional Warranty Terms:

  • Warranty applies only to the original customer and vehicle
  • Warranty claims must be reported within 30 days
  • Vehicle must not be repaired or altered by another party after service
  • No additional warranties are expressed or implied
  • Not responsible for towing, rental vehicles, downtime, or incidental damages
